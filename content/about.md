India's cities are not concrete deserts. Leopards patrol housing colony boundaries
in Mumbai. Peafowl display in Gurugram's glass-tower campuses. Sloth bears wander
onto NH-44 in Hyderabad. Otters fish in Chennai's backwaters.

**Urban Wildlife India** documents these encounters — not as anomalies, but as
evidence of an extraordinary co-existence that most of us pass every day without
noticing.

All photographs are shot in available light, from public access areas, with
minimal disturbance to the animals. No baiting. No flash. No fences.
