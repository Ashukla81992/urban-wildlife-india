While peacocks are most famous for the dramatic fan display of breeding season, this shot captures a different quality — the bird at home in tall vegetation, moving with surprising stealth through dense reeds at the edge of an urban lake.

The cobalt iridescence of the neck appears almost artificially saturated against the muted greens of the reed stems, and the complex scalloped pattern of the mantle feathers is visible in full detail. Peafowl are strong swimmers and are often found near water, a fact that surprises most people.
